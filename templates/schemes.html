Government Farmer Schemes

Explore important government schemes created to support farmers with financial aid, insurance protection, registration services, and agricultural development programs. These schemes help improve farming productivity, income stability, and rural growth.

Kisan Credit Card Scheme

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) provides farmers with easy access to short-term credit for agriculture needs such as seeds, fertilizers, equipment, and other farming expenses. It helps farmers get loans at affordable interest rates.

PM Kisan KYC Scheme

PM-KISAN beneficiaries can complete their KYC verification to continue receiving financial support directly into their bank accounts. This process ensures secure and verified payments for eligible farmers.

New Farmer Registration

New farmers who are eligible under PM-KISAN can register online to receive government income support benefits. Registration helps farmers join official assistance programs quickly and easily.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

This crop insurance scheme protects farmers against crop losses caused by natural disasters, pests, diseases, and unexpected weather conditions. It reduces risk and provides financial security during difficult seasons.

More Ministry Schemes

Visit the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare portal to explore additional government schemes, subsidies, welfare programs, and agriculture development initiatives for farmers across India.
